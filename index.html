Building Women-Led Economies from the Ground Up

A flagship initiative by the Centre for Leadership Coaching, Shoolini University, in collaboration with the Shoolini Pehal Foundation—designed to enable rural women to build sustainable livelihoods, strengthen economic independence, and lead change within their communities.

Learn More

Our Mission

Project Pragati is dedicated to empowering rural women by providing them with the essential entrepreneurship training and support to establish and grow their own businesses. Our unique three-tier mentorship model connects participants (Sakhis) with personalized coaches (Sarthis) and student mentors (Sahyogis) to ensure comprehensive guidance, skill enhancement, and confidence-building for lasting success.
