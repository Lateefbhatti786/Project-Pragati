Priya Sharma

Handmade Crafts Entrepreneur

"Project Pragati Changed Everything for Me"

Priya was a homemaker in Himachal Pradesh with a passion for handicrafts. She lacked the confidence and business knowledge to turn her passion into a sustainable livelihood. When Project Pragati reached her village, she enrolled in the entrepreneurship training program.

"Project Pragati didn't just teach me business skills; it gave me the courage to lead and inspire in my community. Today, I'm not just running a business – I'm mentoring other women."

Anita Patel

Organic Spices Producer

"From Kitchen to Marketplace"

Anita had been making organic spice blends in her kitchen for years, but had no way to scale or market her products. Her family encouraged her, but she doubted if she could manage a real business. Project Pragati gave her the training, mentorship, and support to transform her passion into a profitable enterprise.

"From my small kitchen to a growing business, I am now a self-reliant entrepreneur supporting my family's future. My children see their mother as a businesswoman, not just a homemaker."

Sneha Verma

Fashion Designer & Tailor

"Tailoring Success into Financial Independence"

Sneha was a skilled tailor but worked for minimal wages under others. She dreamed of having her own workshop but lacked the business acumen and confidence. Project Pragati's comprehensive mentorship program helped her create a business plan, secure funding, and launch her own tailoring and design business.

"The mentorship I received was life-changing. I have the confidence to manage my finances, negotiate with clients, and grow my workshop. Now I'm showing my daughter that she can be independent."
