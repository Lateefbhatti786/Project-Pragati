Mentorship Model

Project Pragati employs a unique three-tier mentorship model to ensure comprehensive support for every entrepreneur (Sakhi):

🎯 Sakhis (Entrepreneurs)

Rural women participants receiving training and business setup support

👨‍🏫 Sarthis (Coaches)

Professional mentors providing personalized coaching and guidance

🤝 Sahyogis (Student Mentors)

University students providing peer mentorship and skill development
