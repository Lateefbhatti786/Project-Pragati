Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Project Pragati is a registered NGO and all donations are tax-deductible under section 80G of the Income Tax Act.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, digital wallets, and cheques. All donations are secure and encrypted.

Can I make a monthly donation?

Absolutely! Monthly donations help us plan better and support more entrepreneurs through consistent funding.

Will I receive updates on how my donation is used?

Yes, we provide quarterly impact reports and updates on the entrepreneurs your donation helped.

Can I donate in the name of someone else?

Yes, you can make a donation in honor or memory of someone special and receive a dedicated recognition letter.
